AG
Akash G.
Founder & CEO, Apex Campus Careers

Akash founded Apex Campus Careers in February 2026 with a singular vision: to transform the way college students transition into industry. Recognizing a critical gap between academic preparation and workplace readiness, he built a 360-degree training ecosystem that combines technical depth, aptitude mastery, and professional soft skills — redefining what "career-ready" truly means.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between classroom theory and corporate reality by delivering structured, industry-aligned training that empowers every student to confidently step into their first professional role.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted campus-to-career platform — where every student, regardless of college tier, has equal access to world-class professional training and real industry experience.

The 360° Ecosystem

Aptitude + Technical + Career Readiness training, combined with real internship projects and personalized mentorship — all under one roof.
