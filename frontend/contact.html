FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for?

Apex Campus Careers is designed for college students (1st year to final year) across all branches who want to build industry-relevant skills and secure good placements at top companies.

Is the training online or offline?

We offer a hybrid model — both online and offline. You can attend live sessions from anywhere and also visit our training center if you're local.

What certificate will I receive?

Upon completion of training or internship modules, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate from Apex Campus Careers for your resume and LinkedIn profile.

How do I enroll for an internship?

Simply fill out the contact form above. Our counsellors will reach out to help you pick the right domain and guide you through enrollment.

Are there payment plan options?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans including EMI options. Contact us at apexcampuscareers@gmail.com to discuss what works best for you.

What kind of projects will I work on?

Each internship domain includes 2 Major Projects and 1 Minor Project — all industry-grade, real-world projects you can showcase in your portfolio.
